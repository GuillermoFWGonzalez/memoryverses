Proverbs 10:4-6 & 19

4 He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich. 5 He that gathereth in summer is a wise son: but he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame. 6 Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

19In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin: but he that refraineth his lips is wise.
